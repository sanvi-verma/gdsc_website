CORE TEAM
Driving Growth Of SIT's GDSC

The core team of SIT Pune's GDSC drives the club's mission, organizing events and initiatives to enhance technical skills and foster innovation among students. They actively engage with the student body, solicit feedback, and cultivate strategic partnerships to expand the club's reach. Providing mentorship and guidance, they facilitate members' exploration of Google technologies. Additionally, the core team oversees marketing efforts to promote club events and attract new talent, ultimately contributing to the GDSC community's growth and success at SIT Pune.
